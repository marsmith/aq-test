Aquifer Test Locator
  •  About the application
  •  Disclaimer

The U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) Aquifer Test Locator is a web-based tool for the retrieval of aquifer-test information stored in the National Water Information System (NWIS). The aquifer-test information includes well and aquifer completions, test types and analysis methods, and estimated hydraulic properties. The tool also provides links to associated interpretative publications and hydraulic-test data stored in NWIS or published as data releases.

Since 1994, the USGS Water Mission Area has required internal review and archival of hydraulic-test data and analysis to support estimates of hydraulic properties used in its publications. This tool provides a system to readily serve the estimated aquifer properties and associated test information in support of USGS groundwater investigations.

Developed by Martyn Smith of the NY WSC.
